Pasta

Description

Pasta Carbonara is probably the best and easiest dish you can make. Below is the recipe:

Ingredients

    Pasta
    Bacon
    Oyster mushrooms
    Parmesan
    Butter

Steps

    Start boiling salted water
    Fry bacon in separate pan
    Remove bacon when fried and add in diced oyster mushrooms. Add Pasta to boiling water
    Caramelize mushrooms and cook pasta until just underdone
    Add bacon back into pan along with the pasta and a ladel of water. Continue cooking until pasta done
    Add parmesan and butter to taste
